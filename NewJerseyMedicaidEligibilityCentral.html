New Jersey Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of NJ FamilyCare, the fiftieth and final app in this series: a statutory migration filed by an entire population in one week, the payer who stands in front of Medicaid, and a Medicare register where both programs land in one organization’s hands. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.
