South Carolina Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the files that carry both — 3,000 synthetic members of South Carolina Medicaid: a child assessed as a household of one, parents whose coverage ends on someone else’s birthday, and a Medicare register where the door opens partway — onto a premium ladder whose enrollment rides a file older than X12. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

South Carolina Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes. All members, identifiers, spans, and files are synthetic, generated in the browser from a fixed seed (803001); no PHI, no network calls, no storage.
