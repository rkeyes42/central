Iowa Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of Iowa Medicaid: everything privatized in one day, a carousel of carrier exits the members never chose, a waiver that ended retroactive coverage so the dates agree, and a Medicare register where the series’ signature pair simply does not file. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Iowa Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes.
