Wyoming Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of Wyoming Medicaid, the smallest program in the nation and the last app in this series: a federal match that rides each claim, a provider network that points across the border, and the forty-fourth and final answer to what happens when Medicare arrives. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Wyoming Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes. All members, identifiers, spans, and files are synthetic, generated in the browser from a fixed seed (307001); no PHI, no network calls, no storage.
