Maine Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of MaineCare: an expansion ordered by the voters and granted by the courts, income that comes in with the lobster boats and takes the coverage out with it, and a Medicare register where the only loud file goes to the pharmacy. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Maine Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes. All members, identifiers, spans, and files are synthetic, generated in the browser from a fixed seed (207001); no PHI, no network calls, no storage.
