Kentucky Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of Kentucky Medicaid: a front door built, demolished, and rebuilt; churn with a release number; a waiver vacated the day before launch that scarred the notes but never the spans; and a Medicare register where the arrival rarely waits for the birthday. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Kentucky Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes. All members, identifiers, spans, and files are synthetic, generated in the browser from a fixed seed (502001); no PHI, no network calls, no storage. Policy landscape described as of early 2026; verify currency before relying on any detail.
