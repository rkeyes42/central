Georgia Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of Georgia Medicaid: the only running work requirement in the nation, a coverage gap stored honestly as members with zero spans, a foster-care plan built so placements can move without coverage moving, and a door that opens — for some, for the first time — at 65. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Georgia Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes. All members, identifiers, spans, and files are synthetic, generated in the browser from a fixed seed (404001); no PHI, no network calls, no storage. Policy landscape described as of early 2026; verify currency before relying on any detail.
