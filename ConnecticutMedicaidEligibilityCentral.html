Connecticut Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of Connecticut Medicaid: the only state that ever left managed care, a delivery table whose meaning changed while its columns did not, coverage split by a funding flag no span carries, and a Medicare register where nothing files because there was never a plan to leave. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Connecticut Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes.
