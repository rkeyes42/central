Delaware Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of Delaware Medicaid: thirty years of statewide managed care in three counties, a language the code list cannot name, homecomings that file as nothing, and a Medicare register where the arrival adds a second open enrollment beside the first. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.

Delaware Medicaid Eligibility Central — a single-file demonstration by Robert Thomas Keyes. All members, identifiers, spans, and files are synthetic, generated in the browser from a fixed seed (302001); no PHI, no network calls, no storage. Policy landscape described as of early 2026; verify currency before relying on any detail.
