West Virginia Medicaid Eligibility Central

Eligibility, enrollment, and the file that carries both — 3,000 synthetic members of West Virginia Medicaid: a program covering three in ten of everyone, grandchildren whose addresses change while nothing else does, terminations that happen at the state line, and a Medicare register where the 024 files and nothing follows it. All data is seeded and deterministic; nothing here is real.
